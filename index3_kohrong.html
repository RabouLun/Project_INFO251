Top Place to Visit at Sihanoukville

Koh Rong

Lun Rabou Th 28, 2022

Koh Rong (Koh Touch) Island is a Cambodian island located about 40 kilometers off the coast of Sihanoukville in the Gulf of Thailand. In reality, the island is desolate, underdeveloped, and unexplored. It's a hidden island. However, it will soon become one of Cambodia's most popular tourist destinations. The island is without a doubt the most picturesque in the Kompong Som Region. A snowdrift bay spans for kilometers, surrounded by crystal clear and blue water. A jungle with hundreds of coconut trees and waterfalls invades the island in the middle. On Koh Rong, paradise was discovered.

There are two ferry choices on Koh Rong. The slow boat departs 2-3 times every day and travels for 2 1/2 hours. (As of February 2014, the round-trip fare was $5/10$ with an open return.) The rapid boat takes 40 minutes to an hour and departs twice daily. (R/T $20 with open return) The websites of three fast boat operators, Speedferry Cambodia, Sea Cambodia, and Buva Sea, provide up-to-date information on destinations and timings.
