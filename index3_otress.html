Top Place to Visit at Sihanoukville

Otres Beach

Lun Rabou Th 28, 2022

Otres Beach is the next beach south of Ochheuteal Beach and is similar in many aspects to Ochheuteal - a three-kilometer crescent of near-white sand facing southwest. Otres, on the other hand, is significantly less developed and touristed than Ochheuteal, giving a more calm, easygoing ambiance, and gained the attention of Forbes this year, being included among the Top 22 Beaches in Asia. Though much of the beach was recently closed for a construction project, more than a kilometer of it is still accessible and has the same laid-back vibe. On Otres, there are no hotels, spas, or huge restaurants; instead, a line of beach-shack bars, restaurants, and bungalows stretches down the sand from the junction to the new construction project.

Otres Village was an easygoing backpacker zone inland from Otres Beach until it was swept up in 2019 by a megacity development building to the east of Otres. A few guesthouses exist, but the most are simply serving out their leases.

Otres Beach Resort is in Mittakpheap, a suburb of Sihanoukville, and is close to the entertainment zone as well as the beach. While anybody may appreciate the natural beauty of Ream National Park and Otres Beach, those searching for an action should visit Sihanoukville Port.
