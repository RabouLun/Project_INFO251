Siemreap

Siem Reap is likely Cambodia's most well-known city. This location, which was formerly the capital of the ancient Khmer Empire, is now the country's most popular tourist destination. From the 9th through the 15th century, this friendliest place was home to the Khmer Empire's several capitals.

Phnom Penh

The capital of Cambodia, Phnom Penh, is the biggest and wealthiest city in Cambodia. The city, which is surrounded by water, never runs out of freshwater and natural wealth. If you prefer to tour Phnom Penh, you will find a wide range of temples, palaces, and remains from French colonial past.

Sihanoukville

Sihanoukville, commonly known as Kampong Som, is the capital of Preah Sihanouk Province and a seaside city in Cambodia. This town provides travelers with the ideal combination of beautiful sand beaches, wonderful seafood restaurants, and impressive possibilities for unwinding at pubs.
